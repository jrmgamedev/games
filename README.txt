Cats And Boxes

A casual game inspired by the classic favorite board game — Snake and Ladders.

Features:
• Turn-Based Gameplay
• 8 Awesome Cats
• 5 Unique Maps
• Play Solo or Multiplayer

Note: Multiplayer can be played by sharing a single device.

Maps:
• Tiny Island
• Rocky Mountain
• Spooky Garden
• Ocean Park
• Classic Tower

Supported Languages:
• English
• Español
• 日本語
• 한국어
